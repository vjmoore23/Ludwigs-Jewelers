FAQ's

  1. How long has Ludwigs Jewelers been in business for?

  2. Who are the owners of Ludwigs Jewelers?

  3. Ludwigs Jewelers carries only jewelry. True or False.

  4. Ludwigs Jewelers provides repair services. True or False.

Answers:

  1. Ludwigs Jewelers has been in business for over 140 years.

  2. Ludwigs Jewelers is owned and operated by Ann Wagner, Jane Mentzer, and Emily Dentler.

  3. Ludwigs Jewelers has a wide variety of inventory that includes jewelry, gift ware, watches, clocks, silverware, jewelry boxes, and customized gifts.

  4. Ludwigs Jewelers provides repair services for almost any type of jewelry and giftware in the store.
